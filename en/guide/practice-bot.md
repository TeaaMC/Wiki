# Practice Bot

Practice Bot lets you train Crystal PvP on demand without waiting for another player.

## Open the menu

Join the server and run:

```text
/bot
```

The menu lets you select equipment and available training options. Its layout may change as new modes are added.

![TeaaMC Practice Bot menu](/train-bot.jpg)

*The in-game Practice Bot configuration menu.*

::: tip Training advice
Start with familiar equipment, isolate one skill at a time, then increase the difficulty. Short and consistent sessions are usually more effective than one very long session.
:::
