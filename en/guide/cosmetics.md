# Cosmetics & Tag

Cosmetics are the purely-for-looks stuff — trails when you sprint, effects when you shoot a bow or get a kill, a little pet that follows you, and tags shown next to your name in chat. None of it affects gameplay.

There are two menus that work together: **`/shop`** to buy things, and **`/cosmetics`** to equip what you own.

## Buying at the shop — `/shop`

Run `/shop` to open the main store. Each slot here leads into one type of cosmetic.

![TeaaMC main shop](/images/cosmetics/shop-main.jpg)

Cosmetics are bought with **Shards** — the currency you earn from [Crystal Royale](/en/guide/crystal-royale) (winning, getting kills, or just taking part). Each item shows its price in its lore; if you have enough Shards a click buys it, otherwise it'll let you know.

## Cosmetic types

### Sprint Effect
A trail left behind you while you sprint — fire, smoke, petals, and so on.

![Sprint Effect Shop](/images/cosmetics/sprint-shop.jpg)

### Armor Effect
A glow or sparkle around the armor you're wearing.

![Armor Effect Shop](/images/cosmetics/armor-shop.jpg)

### Arrow Effect
The trail your arrows leave when you shoot a bow.

![Arrow Effect Shop](/images/cosmetics/arrow-shop.jpg)

### Kill Effect
An effect that bursts out when you take down an opponent.

![Kill Effect Shop](/images/cosmetics/kill-shop.jpg)

### Pet
A small pet that follows you around.

![Pet Shop](/images/cosmetics/pet-shop.jpg)

There's also an **Elytra Effect** (a trail while you're flying an elytra) — open it from the Elytra slot in `/shop`.

## Equipping cosmetics — `/cosmetics`

Once you've bought something, run `/cosmetics` to open your wardrobe. This menu has exactly 7 slots, one per type: Sprint, Armor, Arrow, Elytra, Kill, Pet and Tag. Clicking a type opens the list of items you own in that category.

In each list:

- An item you **own** — click it to equip.
- The item you're **currently wearing** is marked; click it again to take it off.
- An item you **don't own yet** is greyed out with a note to buy it in `/shop`.

## Tag

A tag is the little label shown next to your name in chat. It's just another cosmetic, so buying and wearing it works exactly like above.

**Buying a tag:** open `/shop` and pick the **Tag Shop** slot. There are a lot of tags, so they're split across pages — flip through them with the arrow in the corner. Each tag has a preview so you can see it first, and a price in Shards.

![Tag Shop](/images/cosmetics/tag-shop.jpg)

**Wearing a tag:** open `/cosmetics` and pick the **Tag** slot. Click a tag you own to wear it. To remove it, click the tag you're wearing again, or hit the **Unequip** button.

::: tip Where do Shards come from?
Shards mostly come from [Crystal Royale](/en/guide/crystal-royale). Play a few rounds, stack up some shards, then come back and grab the cosmetic or tag you like.
:::
